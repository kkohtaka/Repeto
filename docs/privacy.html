Privacy Policy

Repeto

Last Updated: November 2, 2025

1. Introduction

Repeto ("the App") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what information the App collects and how it is used.

2. Information We Collect

The App collects the following information:

  • Task Data: Names, intervals, completion dates, and other data related to tasks you create
  • iCloud Data: The above task data is stored in your iCloud account for cross-device synchronization

Information We Do NOT Collect:

  • Personally identifiable information (name, email address, etc.)
  • Location data
  • Contacts
  • Photos or camera access
  • Any data not directly related to the App's functionality

3. How We Use Your Information

The collected information is used solely for the following purposes:

  • Sending task reminder notifications
  • Synchronizing data across your devices via iCloud
  • Providing and improving the App's functionality

4. Data Storage

Task data is stored on your device and in your iCloud account. The developer does not have access to your task data. All data is tied to your Apple ID and protected according to Apple's security standards.

5. Third-Party Sharing

The App does not share, sell, or disclose your information to third parties. iCloud synchronization is provided by Apple, and Apple's privacy policy applies to that service.

6. Data Deletion

You can delete tasks at any time within the App. Uninstalling the App will remove all data from your device. Data stored in iCloud can be deleted through your iCloud settings.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Security

The App uses security features provided by Apple to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated as needed. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our GitHub repository Issues page:

https://github.com/kkohtaka/Repeto/issues
